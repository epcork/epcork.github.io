About us

Nestled amidst the picturesque landscape of Blairsville, Georgia, Emerald Golf Club stands as a beacon of luxury and tranquility. Founded over three decades ago by a group of passionate golf enthusiasts, the club has since evolved into a premier destination for both avid golfers and those seeking an escape into nature's embrace. Surrounded by the majestic Blue Ridge Mountains and lush greenery, the club offers a serene ambiance that captivates visitors from the moment they arrive.

With meticulous attention to detail, the golf course at Emerald Golf Club boasts unparalleled quality and design. Crafted by renowned architects, every fairway and green is meticulously maintained to provide golfers with an unforgettable playing experience. From challenging holes that test the skill of even the most seasoned players to scenic vistas that inspire awe, each round at Emerald Golf Club is a journey through natural beauty and sporting excellence.

Beyond its world-class golf facilities, Emerald Golf Club embraces a culture of luxury and hospitality. The clubhouse exudes elegance, offering members and guests a refined retreat where they can unwind after a day on the course. Whether indulging in a rejuvenating spa treatment, lounging by the poolside oasis, or enjoying a gourmet meal at the club's exquisite restaurant, every moment at Emerald Golf Club is infused with opulence and relaxation.

Indulging the senses further, the restaurant at Emerald Golf Club tantalizes the taste buds with a menu crafted from the finest ingredients. From succulent steaks and fresh seafood to decadent desserts, each dish is prepared with precision and passion, reflecting the culinary expertise of the club's renowned chefs. Whether savoring a leisurely meal with friends or celebrating a memorable occasion, dining at Emerald Golf Club is a culinary journey that complements the beauty of its surroundings and the excellence of its golfing experience.
